Popular DL libraries:
    - tensorflow
    - pytorch
    - keras
    - also theano; but not as possible 

tensorflow: 
    most popular, made by google 
pytorch: 
    competitor to tensorflow, supports gpu
    used in academic research 
    used at meta 

keras:
    easier than tensorflow and pytorch 
    API for fast development 
    complex DL with few lines 

    normally runs on top of low level like tensorflow 
    also supported by google
